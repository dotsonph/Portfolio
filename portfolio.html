PORTFOLIO / BLOG / PERSONAL DUMP


You found me! For some background, I recently graduated from George Mason University last May with a B.S Computer Engineering degree
with a concentration in Embedded Systems. Throughout my undergrad, I focused on Embedded Software Development and FPGAs.
In other words, a lot of work with different microprocessors/development boards paired with Xilinx Vivado, Linux Shells, and various IDEs.
Although most of my coursework prioritized computer architecture and lower-level programming, I enjoy really all electrical/software disciplines.

This includes RF, Electronics, Kernel Driver Development, GPU Programming, Ethical Hacking, Video Game Development... You get the point.
I simply loathe the idea of boxing myself in when it comes to a professional career and any particular path. I suppose I am just an unsatisfied learner.
Realistically, I would love to work anywhere with long-term opportunity to grow and career flexibility in firmware and/or software development. Having
said everything, I am by no means opposed to pursuing other fields such as RF engineering, cybersecurity, AI/ML, etc... Who knows what will happen!

Now what am I working on you may ask? Well, this website for one. Given my background, I had zero exposure to frontend development in my undergrad.
I might have been exposed to a scripting language early on in my highschool days, however, none of that knowledge was retained. Not a single line or bracket.
I can actually show below the first HTML document with trash and jargon I compiled while learning just last week. It is safe to say there has been progress.

So I basically spent time after graduating trying to hammer up this portfolio and a half-decent GitHub /** more on this for later **/.
Thankfully a few tutorials and blogs had me up and running in days, learning HTML, CSS, and some extremely simple Javascript. Eventually I want
to learn popular, accomodating frameworks like React, Vue.js, Vite, Tailwind CSS, and others I missed. The issue is sometimes I get caught in the habit of
not starting something just because I feel inadequately equipped to create something "great". And that is why it took me till now to start at all...
Basically, that will be my excuse if this website of mine appears occasionally half-baked while I do my regular tweaks.
There is a lot for me to learn and implement overtime that is.
