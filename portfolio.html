PORTFOLIO / BLOG / PERSONAL DUMP


You found me! For some background, I recently graduated from George Mason University last May with a B.S Computer Engineering degree
with a concentration in Embedded Systems. Throughout my undergrad, I focused on Embedded Software Development and FPGAs.
In other words, a lot of work with different microprocessors/development boards paired with Xilinx Vivado, Linux Shells, and various IDEs.
Although most of my coursework prioritized computer architecture and lower-level programming, I enjoy really all electrical/software disciplines.

This includes RF, Electronics, Kernel Driver Development, GPU Programming, Ethical Hacking, Video Game Development... You get the point.
I simply loathe the idea of boxing myself in when it comes to a professional career and any particular path. I suppose I am just an unsatisfied learner.
Realistically, I would love to work anywhere with long-term opportunity to grow and career flexibility in firmware and/or software development. Having
said everything, I am by no means opposed to pursuing other fields such as RF engineering, cybersecurity, AI/ML, etc... Who knows what will happen!
